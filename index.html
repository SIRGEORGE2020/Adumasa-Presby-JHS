CONSTITUTION

PREAMBLE :

We, the members of THE 2007/2008 YEAR GROUP OF ADUMASA PRESBY JHS, to fully realize our purpose, do hereby adopt and establish this constitution as the guiding instrument of our Association.

ARTICLE 1

Name of Association The name of the Association shall be “2007/2008 YEAR GROUP OF ADUMASA PRESBY JHS” hereafter referred to as THE GROUP 07/08.

ARTICLE 2

Mission and Objective
Section 1
Mission Statement
The mission of THE GROUP 07/08 is to provide support to our former school and also to seek the welfare of the members. To connect with each other to help promote, and develop the life of each other.

Section 2

Objetive(s)
The objective of the association shall be:
  • to help bring all the members who attended or completed Adumasa Presby JHS during the 2007/2008 year group under one umbrella to foster unity and cohesion.
  • to help provide information and assistance to all members in times of need.
  • to connect and find ways to help our alma mater (Adumasa Presby JHS).

    • ARTICLE III

    Membership
    Section 1.Membership in the THE GROUP 07/08 shall be limited to all persons who attended or completed Adumasa Presby JHS in the said year.

    Section 2.Membership in the THE GROUP 07/08 shall not be limited to persons based on their race, color, religion, gender, national origin, age, ability, gender expression, gender identity or sexual orientation.

    Section 3. Active membership in the THE GROUP 07/08 shall be conferred upon those members who, in addition to the above requirements, have paid the monthly dues in full, attend meetings regularly and adheres to the groups rules and regulations.

    ARTICLE IV

    Officers
    Section 1.Section 2. The elected officers of the Association shall consist of leadership roles, for example – President, Vice President, Secretary, Public Relations Officer, Assistant Secretary, Organizer(s) and Treasurer, which shall serve as the Executive Committee. Other positions should be by appointments. An elected officer that wishes to resign must give tangible reason in writing to the executive board and copy the executive committee. An official resignation letter should be submitted at least 3 months before resigning. Section 4. Vetting All members seeking to serve as officers of the Association must undergo a vetting process. Membership of the vetting committee shall comprise all the sitting executive committee members and chaired by the vice president. Section 5. Elections: The President in consultation with the executive committee shall appoint a committee to oversee electoral processes. An existing executive who seeks to contest for another term needs to resign a month before the next electioneering season. Interested members seeking for leadership positions must pick an application form. All officers shall be elected during the annual meeting and shall serve one-year term. All officers shall be elected by a majority of the votes cast. Only active and members in good standing are eligible to vote. If no candidate for an office receives a majority of the votes cast, then only the top two candidates will be placed on a second ballot for that office until a majority is reached. A quorum of two-thirds of the active membership shall be required for all meetings where officer nominations or elections take place. All elections shall be conducted by secret ballot.
